Colours

When thinking about what colours to use for a product that's advertised for women, pink will always be mentioned. It's associated with glamour, which is what this business is promoting and advertising towards its viewers. It was used alongside a light grey background, to allow the pink to have a contrast and point the users to the sections that the client deemed important.

Typography

Two serif fonts were used here, the body text got a basic style, while the headers got a more stylised serif to fit in with the aesthetics feeling throughout the design.

Design Patterns

Accordion. The accordion was used to display a note to the user that couldn't fit onto the section without ruining the layout of it. With what I know now, this is the incorrect way to use an accordion and could have instead been used as a notice at the top of the section.
