Project Overview

Problems

The main problem for this design was keeping to the theme of sci-fi and creating something that used lines with black and white blending into each other.

Colours

Due to the focus being in outer space, the majority of the colours are going to be neutral with some bright colours to draw attention to the call to actions as well as to create an exciting environment to showcase action within the game and cool points of interest to interact with. Light accents are used to create a sci-fi effect, as if the user is viewing it on a futuristic data pad but to also contain multiple sections on the page.

Typography

For the typography, I used a font that had characters that stood out on their own and had a strong showing when capitalised. Not much text is being used throughout the design but the small amount that is, it needed to stand out against the background and imagery.

Design Patterns

The main design pattern here are the small grid galleries. They do need to be revisited due to all 3 having slight differences to them, but having the light accent border allows the images to have their own little space while giving it that "pop" to stand out amongst the others.
