This was my first ever attempt at creating some form of template. The aim of this template was to create something for a video game streamer on the popular streaming platform twitch. They could use this site to promote themselves even more and have a collection of all their content in one area. The main focus would be the stream and video content so not much will be done for colours and typography.

Project Overview

Problems

The main problem for this design was to create some form of personality that matched the streamer and their brand. I needed to being able to use large imagery to promote themselves and their sponsors whilst also drawing attention to the streamers live sessions and socials.

Users - Month

Colours

There wasn't much focus on colours in this template. Neutral colours were used to make certain images / banners stand out more to the user.

Typography

There was also not much focus on the typography, a basic font-style was used as well as the weight and colour of the fonts.

Design Patterns

Design patterns weren't really being focused on with this design, I was more of less just putting pieces together for what a typical design would be like for a streamer. A product page was used for this design, featuring a filter. Since the main focus is the stream itself, not a lot of attention was given to the pattern but just enough to give the user enough infomation if they happen to come across it or visit the page.
