Project Overview

Problems

The problem for this design was the colour and theme of the site. Since this was for people looking to arrange a funeral service, I needed to find a way to use colours that soothed the user and to make the company feel reliable to handle their loved ones appropriately and with respect.

Colours

The colours were taken from their shop banner. Being a marigold type of colour, it makes the website feel less depressing, uplifts the users and offers hope. It's partnered with white, to keep with the flow of bright colours, however due to it being one of my early designs, I used the pure white colour which is a little too bright.

Typography

Not much attention was given to the typography in this design. A common serif font was used alongside increased font size, weight and style.

Design Patterns

The carousel was one of my very first tries at designing and implementing a carousel for a website. Its very basic and not very attractive but it did its job at being able to display multiple pictures of their business within its section.
