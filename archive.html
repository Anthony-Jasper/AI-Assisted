Cloudyne Limited

Cloudyne was a business that focused on providing small businesses with up-to-date websites with the latest trends. Websites can be expensive and can come as a shock to some people, so they provided them with reasonable prices for the size of their business. Alongside websites, Cloudyne offered domain and hosting with no increase on recurring pricing.

Helene Simpson Photography

Helene Simpson Photography was a client's website that focused on selling photographs of newborns to create memories for the families as their child/children grow up. This was a website that needed to use a lot of imagery to capture the user's gaze and to make a purchase at the end of it.

Medway Vegan Festivals

Medway Vegan Festivals website was meant for a way for people to find out about local festivals and to organise stalls or book tickets to attend them. Production for this website stopped only a few days into the design phase because the client was unsure if they were able to manage the site alongside their other activities. In the images below you will see some low and high-fidelity mixed together.

Student Project

The purpose of this design was to help a student that was studying web design in college who asked me for some help. My aim was to show her how to separate out sections of the site, how colours are important to bring attention and how to get the user to view the site you intended them to.

Pout & Sparkle

Pout & Sparkle is a website that promotes and sells aesthetics. Since launch, this website has gone through many re-designs and tweaks due to the client having closed and restarted the business, whilst also being indecisive with what they wanted on their website. With this design I wanted to create something that appealed to females and a focus on imagery.

Shea-Me

Shea-Me was my first ever attempt at doing any sort of web design with no prior experience going into it. I used a lot of inspiration from other websites and did an extensive amount of research on what to include in an ecommerce website. The website's purpose is to sell scented shea body butters through their online shop.
