Colours

In colour theory, blue is often represented as trust and loyalty. This is something we wanted to project to our clients when they viewed our business page. For the secondary colour, I went with the complementary colour to blue, which is orange. This allowed the two to stand out and allow the user to recognise immediately what was required of them when viewing a certain element on the page.
